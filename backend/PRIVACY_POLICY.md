# Privacy Policy for GroceNest

**Last Updated: March 19, 2026**

At GroceNest, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share your personal information.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Personal Data**: Name, email, phone number, and delivery address.
- **Payment Information**: Processed securely via Stripe (we do not store raw card details).
- **Usage Data**: Route activity, IP address, and device metadata for performance and analytics.

## 2. How We Use Your Data
- To process and deliver your grocery orders.
- To communicate order status updates and notifications.
- To improve our services through analytics and feedback.

## 3. Data Protection (GDPR)
We implement AES-256-GCM encryption for sensitive fields and provide permanent account deletion options to ensure full compliance with GDPR mandates.

## 4. Contact Us
For any privacy-related inquiries, please contact support@grocenest.com.
